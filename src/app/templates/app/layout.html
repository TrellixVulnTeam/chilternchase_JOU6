Chiltern Chase Update COVID 19

It is with deep regret that we have decided to cancel the Chiltern Chase for the first time in 14 years. This is due to current Government advice emphasising our shared responsibility to slow the spread of COVID-19 through "increased social distancing".  This means avoiding unnecessary travel and gatherings of any size. So much organisation goes into such an event and there would not be sufficient time to get everything ready for the day. We hope that you all stay well in these difficult and challenging times.


The Chiltern Chase Committee
